Foundation Rescue

Foundation has shut down. Your token contracts are on-chain and safe - but the art and metadata living on IPFS could disappear when Foundation stops pinning it. This tool pins your content to your own Pinata account and shows you how to retrieve any NFTs locked in the Foundation marketplace contract.
